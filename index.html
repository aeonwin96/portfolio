About

My journey into technical writing started one sunny morning in Logan, Utah, circa 2014. It was graduation day at USU. I recall standing at the metaphorical precipice, clutching my degrees, while projecting a thousand-yard stare directly at the horizon. But despite my earnest attempt at a pomp and circumstance to match the day's ceremony, I was fast aware of a developing inability to focus my gaze past the rhetorical edge, where my promising finance career was calling up from the ravine below.

Later that fateful day, an old friend reached out to me about a job opportunity in SEO and Content Management. The skills I learned there helped me move onto a position managing pools of freelance writers, creating technical content for marketing purposes. Both of those position were the foundation for securing my role in API documentation at Adobe Workfront. During my tenure, I've grown more than at any other time in my career. Now, when I look to the horizon, I'm excited to see all of the opportunities that lie ahead.
